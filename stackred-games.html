Stack Red games

A programming competition for anyone with a knack for programming, solving problems. Participants can compete in teams of maximum 3 members.

The Competition will consist of 2 rounds, Online Prelims, and the final Onsite round. The Online round will be held on 22nd October, 2018.

The Onsite round will be held during Innovision - Tech Fest of NSUT, Delhi.

Top 15 teams from all over India from the Online Prelims will be invited for Onsite finals that shall take place at the NSUT campus where they will again be competing for the title of the ultimate coding team.

Rules

  • Please do not discuss strategy, suggestions or tips in the comments during a live contest. Posting questions clarifying the problem statement is ok. If you are unsure, email us.
  • Discussing problems or any aspect of problem, on any other platform on web, on identification, could lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Almost all languages supported on CodeChef are allowed.
  • Each program will be tested based on our critical test data. However output and input should be exactly as specified in the samples provided
  • The decision of the organizers in declaring the results will be final. No queries in this regard will be entertained.
  • Any participant found to be indulging in any form of malpractice will be immediately disqualified.
