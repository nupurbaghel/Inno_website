Robo Soccer

  • Details
  • Venue
  • Organizers

To Design and build manually operated robots that can perform the Task of dragging and pushing a ball in opponents goal maximum number of times in a limited time.

Rules-

  • • Participants have to build two robots that will compete against the two robots of opponent teams in an one on one soccer match of 3 minutes.
  • • Max 6 members /team. Anyone with a valid college/school id will be allowed to participate.
  • • The match will consist of two halves of 1.5 minutes each and a repair time of 1 min will be provided in between.
  • • Robots can be wired as well as wireless.
  • • Points will be rewarded for each goal and deducted for each foul.
  • • Damaging the arena and intentionally damaging the opponent robot will be counted as a foul and may lead to disqualification.
  • • Intentional holding off the ball will also be counted as a foul.
  • • Max Bot weight- 2.5kg/bot (10% tolerance).
  • • Max Bot dimensions -30x30x30 cm (10% tolerance).
  • • Wire length – greater than 5m.
  • • Max voltage between any two points -24v
    • Arena- 8ftx4ft ply wood (smooth) Width of goal= 45cm
