Details: Placing bets, solving puzzles, earning money. Sounds fun? ASN presents to you, CODE GAMBLE, a betting based event, where your puzzle solving and gambling skills will earn you points and cash ;)
  1. This is an onsite event. Participate as a lone wolf or in a team of 2.
  2. All the teams will be given a certain number of poker chips at the beginning in exchange for the entry fee.
  3. The event will consist of various rounds.
    a) At the start of a round, players will have to wager some chips to maintain play at the table.
    b) A programming question will be displayed on the screen and an initial time limit will be set within which you have to solve the question when someone "calls" for a face off.
    c) If you think your team can solve the question in lesser time, "raise" the bet claiming for better solving time.
    d) If you think you will not be able to solve the question with the current time limit, you can "fold" the round.
    e) Once a team calls for a face off, the timer will be started with the current time limit. Whoever codes the solution fastest, the pot of money for that round becomes theirs. In case no one is able to, pot goes to the house.
  4. There will be bonus questions too, giving you a chance to win your wager back, to double the wager etc.
  5. You can exchange your poker chips for cash anytime you want.
