Hack Ok Please

  • Details
  • Venue
  • Organizers

A 24 hour hackathon where participants put in their brains to ideate something exciting and develop it under the theme of 'Make for India' . Product can be website, application, module etc. Various technologies ranging but not limited to ML, AI, Blockchain, IoT can be incorporated into the their prototype. Hackathon also offers opportunity to Interact with fellow developers, mentors and judges.

Rules-

  • 1)Team size - upto 3 members
  • 2)Teams should be made up exclusively of students who are not organizers, volunteers, judges, sponsors, or in any other privileged position at the event.
  • 3)All team members should be present at the event for the entire duration of the hackathon
  • 4)Teams can of course gain advice and support from organizers, volunteers, sponsors, and others.
  • 5)All work on a project should be done at the hackathon. Teams will be subject to disqualification if found otherwise
  • 6)Teams can work only on ideas that align closely with the theme for the hack
  • 7)Teams can use libraries, frameworks, or open-source code in their projects.
  • 8)Working on a project before the event and open-sourcing it for the sole purpose of using the code during the event is against the spirit of the rules and is not allowed.
  • 9)Adding new features to existing projects is allowed. Judges will only consider new functionality introduced or new features added during the hackathon in determining the winners.
  • 10)Teams must stop hacking once the time is up. However, teams are allowed to debug and make small fixes to their programs after time is up. e.g. If during demoing your hack you find a bug that breaks your application and the fix is only a few lines of code, it's okay to fix that. Making large changes or adding new features is not allowed.
