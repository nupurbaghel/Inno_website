Fintech is nothing new, infact it is as old as finance itself. Finance came into being with currency notes but their very printing was made possible due to a technological breakthrough, the Printing Press. Comes 1868 and the first trans atlantic telegraph line was laid between london and new york and the world started trading securities globally. 1968 finance was digitized. Even today the largest spender on technology is the financial sector.

Fintech companies or Techfin companies have disrupted the way we deal with money. They have made it easier for us to deal with money and at the same time made very complex financial arrangements possible which were not possible earlier, at least for a common man like escrow services, crowd funding and ICOs. This is called Disruption. The FES of NSIT welcomes you to showcase your disruptive idea. It could be anything that make businesses do their businesses better as long as you can explain every aspect of your product.

This is not a coding event, so we encourage participants with ideas but no coding skills to collaborate with coders to give their presentations a technical angle.

  1. How well the target industry/sector/vertical and the problem is defined. - What industry and what segment of that industry i.e. logistics, sales, consumer relations etc. is being catered to and how.
  2. How big disruption can the product cause. - Does your product change the way things are done or is it making a process twice as fast , a quantitative estimate of much things are getting better.
  3. Components used and how scalable they are. - What technologies you have used blockchain, big data, ai, nlp databases and the tools. Why did you choose these tools and how scalable they are i.e. how profitable a big version of your product it.
  4. Design (static view and dynamic views) - technical models preffered The model should make the judges understand how your product work.
