Robo Race

  • Details
  • Venue
  • Organizers

To design a manually operated robot that could travel through various terrains consisting of different hurdles in minimum time.

Rules-

  • • Participants have to build a robot that clear maximum hurdles in minimum time.
  • • Max 6 members /team. Anyone with a valid college/school id will be allowed to participate.
  • • Robots can be wired as well as wireless.
  • • Points will be rewarded for each checkpoint and deducted for each foul.
  • • Maximum time allowed for clearing the track is 5 minutes
  • • Max Bot weight- 3kg/bot (10% tolerance).
  • • Max Bot dimensions -30x30x30 cm (10% tolerance).
  • • Wire length – greater than 5m.
  • • Max voltage between any two points -24v
    • Track- Track might consist of various hurdles such as sand, pebbles, oil, water slopes , Bridges, rollers.
